## Summary

Corrected a stale ownership claim in the `anvilml-server` crate-level doc comment.
The original comment on line 5 stated the crate owns "the artifact store" — a claim
that became false when `ArtifactStore` was relocated to the `anvilml-artifacts` crate
in task P902-A1. The edit removes that clause and restructures the sentence to end
at "WebSocket broadcaster." while preserving the accurate claims about router/handlers
ownership and the "no business logic" hard constraint. The word "artifacts" remains
in the handler parenthetical because the crate owns the artifact HTTP handlers, not
the store itself.
